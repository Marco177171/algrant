Support the project

Algrant is an algorithm free social network. It refuses the usage of AI and algorithms for commercial and political purposes, as well as aiming to deliver a service based on making people meet in real life. Algrant users will only receive contents in cronological order, and only the contents they expressively want to see. This means they will never get contents that were mixed and ordered by an algorithm meant to maximize sales or manipulate opinions. In order to maintain itself, of course, Algrant will need sponsorships. Those who decide to sponsor the project have to keep in mind this fact:

No targeting in this network

If you wish to sponsor the project, know that we won't use any kind of data to target specific people. Your sponsorship appears on the page in random order with the others, and it doesn't appear more or less to any user on the base of his/her position, age, cultural level or ideology.

Having that said:

If you find value in what we're doing, and you want to appear among our supporters, get in touch with us.
